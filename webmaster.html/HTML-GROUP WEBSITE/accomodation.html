~> ACCOMODATION AT PANTAI CHENANG <~



CENANG VIEW HOTEL


Pantai Cenang's fine powdery sand beach, fringed by coconut palms and casuarinas, stretches for 2 kilometres and is lined with resorts, luxury hotels, chalets, and restaurants. During peak season, it serves as a good base for exploring nearby islands such as Pulau Rebak Kecil and Pulau Rebak Besar. Located on Langkawi's south-western tip, about 18 kilometres from Kuah.




BEACH RESORT & SPA, LANGKAWI




view of pelangi beach resort & spa

Pelangi Beach Resort and Spa, located on Langkawi's sandy shores, provides relaxing accommodation in spacious wooden chalets. The resort does have its own beach, four dining options, and two free-form outdoor pools. There is internet Access and free parking. Every unit has a private balcony with views of the sea and beautifully landscaped. They are modernly furnished and include a flat-screen satellite TV and a sofa. The spacious bathroom has a separate bath and shower. Cba serves fresh seafood alfresco, while the rustic Spice Market serves authentic Asian flavours. At the Cascade Pool Bar and Pelangi Lounge, you can enjoy a variety of refreshing beverages accompanied by light snacks. Guests can take advantage of the resort's extensive wellness facilities, which include a swimming pool.
