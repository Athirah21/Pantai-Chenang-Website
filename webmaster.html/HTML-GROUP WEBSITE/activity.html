WATERSPORTS IN PANTAI CENANG

On Cenang beach, there are numerous operators who provide water sports opportunities. Jet ski tours, water ski adventures, kayaking, speed boat joyrides, banana boating, and other activities are available.

    SCUBA DIVING IN PANTAI CENANG

    While most dive operators take you to Pulau Payar Marine Park for a dive, one with a small kiosk right on the beach also offers dives at offshore water locations near Cenang beach and from their own small dive boat.

    KARTING IN PANTAI CENANG

    Pantai Cenang Langkawi has a karting centre. Karting is available on a 1230-meter track. The track has previously hosted two Rotax World Finals. There are one-seater and two-seater karts available, both with four-stroke engines and top speeds of 80 km/h. Helmets and instructions are provided at no cost.
